Disc Press

Turn your own audio files into playable Minecraft music discs. Everything runs locally in your browser, nothing is uploaded anywhere.

4How to get your discs

STEP 1
Trade a librarian
Find a level 5 (master) librarian and trade 16 emeralds plus 4 amethyst shards for a blank disc.
STEP 2
Use a stonecutter
Place the blank disc into any stonecutter. It only works there, it has no other use on its own.
STEP 3
Pick a track
Choose any of your generated discs from the stonecutter's recipe list to cut the blank into it.
STEP 4
Play it
Take the finished disc and drop it into a jukebox. Titles and artists show up in the subtitle when it plays.
The blank disc has no source in survival or the creative menu on its own, it only comes from the librarian trade above, so nothing else a player is holding can accidentally trigger these recipes.
